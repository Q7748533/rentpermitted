Regulatory Overview

Georgia has no state-level STR preemption law, placing full regulatory authority with individual cities. Atlanta requires primary residence for STR operators: the property must be the host's primary home. A $150/year registration fee. Savannah operates a certification system with higher upfront costs ($400 initial / $250 renewal) and zoning-based eligibility. Both cities are viable for owner-occupants, but Georgia offers limited opportunity for pure investment properties.
