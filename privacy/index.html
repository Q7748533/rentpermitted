Privacy Policy

RentPermitted is a static information website. We do not collect, store, or process personal data from visitors.

Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics (GA4) to measure traffic volume and page popularity. This helps us understand which content is most useful to readers. GA4 uses first-party cookies to distinguish visitors and sessions. The data sent to Google includes:

  • Pages viewed and time spent on each page
  • Approximate geographic region (country/city level)
  • Device type and browser
  • Referring source (how you found us)

We do not send any personally identifiable information to Google Analytics. IP addresses are anonymized by default in GA4. We do not use Google's advertising features, remarketing, or audience demographics.

Google processes this data under their own privacy policy. You can opt out of Google Analytics on all websites via the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

Server logs

Vercel, our hosting provider, may maintain standard server access logs for operational purposes. These logs are not accessible to or used by RentPermitted.

Third-party links

Our pages link to external websites (government agencies, data providers). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites.
