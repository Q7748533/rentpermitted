Regulatory Overview

Texas presents a mixed regulatory landscape — some cities welcome STR investment while others impose strict limits. Investors must choose cities carefully. Across 3 cities we cover, the rules range from light-touch registration to primary residence mandates and night caps. Texas has no state-level STR preemption law, but most major cities (Austin, Dallas, Houston) have adopted straightforward registration systems rather than restrictive caps.
