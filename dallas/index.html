Overview

93|

Dallas is one of the most permissive major cities for STRs. No primary residence requirement, no night cap, no density limits — just a $285 annual registration and tax compliance. The catch: platforms do NOT collect hotel occupancy tax in Dallas. Hosts must register with the city and remit taxes directly. This adds administrative burden but doesn't change the underlying permissiveness. Dallas's market is driven by business travel, conventions, and sports tourism (Cowboys, Mavericks, Stars). It's not a leisure destination like Austin or Scottsdale, but the regulatory simplicity makes it one of the easiest cities to operate in.

Is Dallas STR-Friendly?

184|

Dallas is a regulatory paradise for STR investors. No primary residence, no caps, no night limits — just register and remit taxes. The market ADR is lower than leisure destinations, but the regulatory simplicity and scalability make up for it. The HOT self-remittance adds an administrative step (platforms don't collect), but professional management handles this. For investors seeking the path of least regulatory resistance among major cities, Dallas delivers.
