Regulatory Overview

Arizona stands out as an STR-friendly state with regulatory clarity and investor protections. Across all 2 cities we track, reasonable fees, clear rules, and state-level preemption laws make this one of the most predictable markets for short-term rental investment. Arizona's SB1350 prohibits cities from banning short-term rentals outright, providing strong state-level protection for investors. Cities can require licenses and enforce nuisance rules, but cannot restrict STRs by zoning alone.
