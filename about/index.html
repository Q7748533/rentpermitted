About RentPermitted

RentPermitted is an independent resource for short-term rental hosts and investors in the United States. We track permit requirements, tax obligations, operating rules, and enforcement trends across major US cities.

What we cover

  • 25 US cities — from Austin to Washington DC
  • License types and fees — exact dollar amounts from official sources
  • Tax rates — city, county, and state-level obligations
  • Operating rules — noise, occupancy, safety requirements
  • Enforcement risks — penalties and compliance deadlines
  • Investor analysis — independent scorecards and year-1 cost estimates

How we work

Every city page is researched from official government sources (.gov websites, municipal codes, and government memoranda). We cross-verify key data points against multiple sources. Market data comes from AirDNA, AirROI, and StaySTRA. We update pages as regulations change.

What we're not

RentPermitted is not a government agency. We do not issue permits or licenses. We are not affiliated with Airbnb, Vrbo, or any booking platform. Our information is for reference only — always verify with your local government before listing your property.
