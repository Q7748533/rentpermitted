Florida (FL) Short-Term Rental Regulations

Compare STR rules across 2 Florida cities. License fees, tax rates, and operating rules. Verified May 2026.

Regulatory Overview

Florida has some of the most restrictive short-term rental regulations in the country. Across all 2 cities we track, investors face significant barriers including primary residence requirements, night caps, and active enforcement. STR operators must research city-level rules carefully before investing. Florida preempts local governments from banning vacation rentals entirely (FL Stat. 509.032), but cities retain authority over registration, fees, and enforcement.
