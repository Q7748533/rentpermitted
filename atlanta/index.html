Overview

Atlanta has a straightforward STR regulatory environment: $150/year permit, no primary residence requirement, no caps, and platform-collected taxes. The market is driven by business travel (Delta HQ, Fortune 500s), conventions, and sports/entertainment. Atlanta's airport is the busiest in the world, creating constant transient demand. The $150/year permit is affordable, and the regulatory environment is business-friendly — no surprise for a city built on commercial hospitality.
