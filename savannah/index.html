Overview

Savannah requires STR certification for properties in its historic and Victorian districts, with a $400 initial fee and $250 annual renewal. The historic district regulations are layered: zoning determines where STRs can operate, and 3 violations in 12 months trigger automatic certificate revocation. Savannah's tourism (historic squares, SCAD, film tourism) drives strong demand, especially in walkable historic areas. The regulatory system is structured but navigable for compliant operators.

License Application — Step by Step

  1. Verify property zoning allows STR (Historic District / Victorian District)
  2. Apply for STR Certificate ($400 initial)
  3. Register for Hotel/Motel Tax
  4. Display certificate number on listings
  5. Renew annually ($250)
  6. Maintain zero violations (3 in 12 months = revocation)

Taxes

8% Savannah Hotel/Motel Tax + 7% Georgia state sales tax = 15%. Platforms collect.

Key Operating Rules

  • Historic District and Victorian District subject to STR certification
  • 3 violations in 12 months = automatic certificate revocation
  • Hotel/Motel Tax + state sales tax collected by platforms
  • Noise and nuisance strictly enforced in historic neighborhoods
  • Property must meet safety codes

Penalties for Non-Compliance

  • Fine per violation
  • 3 violations in 12 months triggers certificate revocation
